About Us

The Ogden Yoga Festival started in 2011 to help raise money for those who have been a victim of domestic violence by donating 100% of the proceeds to the Ogden YCC. Teachers from all over the area donate their time and talents to contribute to this festival and bring many flavors of Yoga and healthy living for everyone to experience.

Our Goal

To build awareness about yoga in Ogden and the surrounding community, and raise funds for the Family Crisis Center in Ogden.
